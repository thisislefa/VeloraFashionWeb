THE ORIGIN

WEARING THE
ALGORITHM

Founded in 2024, Velora emerged from a simple question: What does the internet look like when you wear it?

We started in a small basement studio, screen-printing glitched graphics onto thrifted hoodies. Today, we are a global collective of designers, coders, and artists obsessed with the future of silhouette and fabric. Our aesthetic is unapologetically brutalist, yet comfortable enough for the apocalypse.
